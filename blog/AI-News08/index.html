ChatGPT Experiences Traffic Decline

ChatGPT experienced a decline in monthly traffic and unique visitors in June, marking its first decrease since its November launch.

What you need to know

  • Worldwide desktop and mobile traffic to the ChatGPT website decreased by 9.7% in June from May, while unique visitors to ChatGPT's website dropped 5.7%.

  • The amount of time visitors spent on the website was also down 8.5%, the data shows.

  • OpenAI released the ChatGPT app on the iOS system in May, which could sap some traffic from its website. The chatbot was downloaded more than 17 million on iOS globally as of July 4.
